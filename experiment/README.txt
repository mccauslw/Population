Supplemental materials for "A population choice experiment for testing axioms of stochastic discrete choice" by William McCausland, Tony Marley and Clint Davis-Stober.

1. all_domains_data.pdf

- Description of all domains of the choice experiment, with screenshots
- Raw experiment data for all domains, with illustrations

2. feedback.pdf

- Complete list of feedback left by respondants

3. Certificate of approval.pdf

- Certificate of ethics approval for Tony Marley from the University of Victoria

4. Consentment_form.pdf

- Consentment form provided to participants in the experiment

5. Recruitment_Compensation_procedures.pdf

- Description of recruitment and compensation procedures used by SSI

6. ssi-2017-panel-book.pdf

- SSI's Global panel book for 2017, the year of the experiment
